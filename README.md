# Voca

> Voice AI for India's helplines — built so being understood comes before being answered.

Voca is a verify-before-act voice AI for India's citizen helplines. It hears the caller in their dialect, restates back what it understood, only then acts — and when distress spikes, it rapid-acks and seamlessly hands off to a human agent with full context.

## Live site

https://github.com/KundanKhatri/voca-public#:~:text=kundankhatri.github.io/voca%2Dpublic/

The site walks through how Voca works for everyone — including a picture-story explainer designed so anyone, regardless of technical background, can understand it in 60 seconds.

## In one paragraph

The biggest failure on a citizen helpline is not lack of response — it is the wrong response, born of the wrong understanding. India has 22 official languages and hundreds of dialects. Most helpline agents speak one or two of them. Voca sits between the citizen and the agent as a voice AI assistive layer, designed so the citizens hardest to hear today are the first ones served tomorrow.

## Status

Voca was developed in the context of **AI for Bharat 2** (May 2026) — co-presented by the PAN IIT Bangalore Alumni Association and the Government of Karnataka — as a response to **Theme 12: AI for the 1092 Helpline** (Department of Personnel & Administrative Reforms, e-Governance, Govt. of Karnataka).
