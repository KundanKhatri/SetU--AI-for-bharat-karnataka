📞 A simple story · made for everyone to understand

When someone calls for help,
they should be heard.

This is the story of Setu — a friendly bridge that helps every person in India be understood when they call a helpline. Even if they speak a different language. Even if they're upset. Even if they're scared.

Setu does three simple things.

No fancy words. Here is the whole product, in three pictures.

It listens carefully

Even if you speak in a special way — your village's Kannada, your dialect, your mix of languages. Setu can hear you.

It checks: "Did I get it?"

Before doing anything, Setu repeats what you said in your own words. You say yes — it acts. You say no — it asks again.

If you're scared, it acts fast

If you're upset or in danger, Setu doesn't wait. It says "Help is coming" and gets a real person on the line in seconds.

A story: Lakshmi calls for help.

Lakshmi is 62. She lives in Mandya. She speaks her own kind of Kannada. Today, she needs help.

01
The story begins

Lakshmi picks up the phone.

She is sad. Her husband is shouting at her. She doesn't know where to go. She dials 1092.

02
Today, without Setu

The helper doesn't speak her dialect.

The agent asks her to repeat. Lakshmi has to calm down and try again. Two precious minutes are lost — at the hardest moment of her week.

03
Now imagine Setu

Setu listens — in Lakshmi's exact way of speaking.

It doesn't matter that her Kannada is from Mandya. Setu has been taught to hear how people in different parts of Karnataka actually talk.

04
It feels too

Setu hears the worry in Lakshmi's voice.

It can tell when someone is scared, in danger, or in pain — by listening to how they speak, not just what they say.

05
It speaks back gently

Setu speaks to Lakshmi, in her own dialect.

It doesn't say a long script. It says the right thing, in the right tone, in the right language — "I hear you. I'm with you. Help is coming."

06
A real person joins — fast

In about 3 seconds, an agent is on the line.

The agent already knows what's wrong, in Lakshmi's own words. The agent already sees that Lakshmi is in distress. The first words spoken are exactly the right ones.

07
Happy ending

Lakshmi gets help. Without having to repeat herself.

That is what Setu does — every minute, every shift, in every language and every dialect of India.
