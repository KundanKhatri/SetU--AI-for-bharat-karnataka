Talk to Voca

Speak. In your language.
Voca will listen, then speak back warmly.

A live conversation built on Sarvam end-to-end — Saarika STT, Sarvam-M LLM for interpretation, Bulbul TTS for response. Real Indic prosody. Real distress and risk scoring on each turn. Designed to feel like a person, not a robot.

One-time setup — paste your Sarvam API key

The key is stored only in your browser (localStorage). It never leaves your device except to call Sarvam directly. You can change or remove it any time.

Don't have a key? Get one at dashboard.sarvam.ai — free trial available. The Saarika STT, Sarvam-M, and Bulbul TTS calls used by this page each cost less than ₹0.10 per turn at typical lengths.
