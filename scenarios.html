Home Architecture Brief

Four people. Four calls.
Two stories per call — one for today, one for tomorrow with Voca.

The 1092 helpline is not an abstraction. Every shift, real Indians dial it from real distress, in their real languages. This page walks through four representative callers — drawn from the actual demographics, dialects, and grievance patterns the helpline serves — and shows what happens to each of them today, and what happens with Voca.

The four callers

Real demographics, real grievance categories, real dialects.

Personas are composite, but every detail — district, dialect, grievance type, time of day — is drawn from the publicly known caller profile of helplines like Karnataka 1092 and the national 112 ERSS.

L
Lakshmi · 62 · Mandya district
Mandya-Kannada speaker · domestic distress
"ನನ್ ಗಂಡ ಮನೇಲಿ ಭಾರಿ ಬಯ್ತಿದಾನ್... ನಾ ಎಲ್ಲಿ ಹೋಗ್ಲಿ?" "My husband is shouting at me at home... where do I go?"
Today
Agent speaks standard Kannada. Mandya verb-form "ಬಯ್ತಿದಾನ್" sounds unfamiliar — agent asks her to repeat. She has to compose herself, repeat the sentence in standard Kannada. 2 minutes lost at the start of a domestic-violence call.
With Voca
Mandya-dialect verb-form is in the acoustic eval set. Sentiment fires distress 0.81 + lexical risk = HIGH. Voca skips the verification turn, says "I hear you. Help is coming." in her dialect, parallel-routes to a women's-cell-trained agent. Agent on the line in 2.5 seconds with full context.
R
Ravi · 28 · Belagavi district
Belagavi-Kannada · ration-card grievance
"ರೇಶನ್ ಕಾರ್ಡ್ ರಿನ್ಯೂ ಆಗ್ಲಿಲ್ಲ ಅಣ್ಣಾ, ಆನ್‌ಲೈನ್‌ಗೆ ಸಪೋರ್ಟ್ ಬೇಕಿತ್ತು" "My ration card hasn't been renewed, sir, I needed help with the online portal."
Today
Code-mixed Kannada+English. Agent isn't sure if "renew" means renewal or replacement. Misroutes to grievance-resolution. Resolution takes 11 days for a 2-day issue.
With Voca
Voca interpretation: intent=ration_card_renewal, entities={portal:online}, conf 0.88. Verifies in his dialect: "ನಿಮ್ ರೇಶನ್ ಕಾರ್ಡ್ ರಿನ್ಯೂ ಮಾಡೋಕೆ ಆನ್‌ಲೈನ್ ಸಹಾಯ ಬೇಕಾ?" He confirms. Routed correctly first time. Resolved in 2 days.
P
Priya · 34 · Bengaluru Urban
Hinglish/English · workplace harassment
"Sir, mere office mein ek senior is harassing me daily. मैं डर के बिना काम कैसे करूं" "Sir, a senior at my office is harassing me daily. How do I work without fear?"
Today
Agent speaks fluent Kannada but limited English; English/Hindi fluency varies. Priya repeats herself, switches between Hindi and English, audio breaks. Sentiment escalates as she feels unheard.
With Voca
Code-mixing detected; LLM handles Hinglish natively. Sentiment = fear + sustained anger. Voca paraphrases empathetically, soft-handover to agent fluent in Hindi+English, full transcript + sentiment timeline on the agent's screen.
I
Imran · 45 · Mangaluru (Dakshina Kannada)
Mangaluru-Kannada + Beary + Urdu · medical emergency
"ನನ್ ಮಗಳಿಗೆ ಶ್ವಾಸಕೋಶ ಸಮಸ್ಯೆ, ಆಂಬ್ಯುಲೆನ್ಸ್ ಬೇಕು... she's only 8 years old" "My daughter has a breathing problem, we need an ambulance... she's only 8 years old."
Today
Coastal Karnataka dialect + emergency English. Agent repeats key terms to confirm. ~90 seconds until ambulance is dispatched. Every second matters in pediatric respiratory emergencies.
With Voca
Acoustic+lexical risk_signal = HIGH ("ಶ್ವಾಸಕೋಶ ಸಮಸ್ಯೆ" + age 8 + ambulance request). Bypass-verification rule fires. Rapid-ack in his dialect, parallel ambulance dispatch through the 108 integration in under 6 seconds.
