The flip

Most voice AI: hear → respond.
Voca: hear → restate → verify → only then act.

The verification turn is not a usability feature. It is the structural rule of the system. No interpretation, however confident, is acted upon without explicit citizen confirmation. There is one exception, and it is the most important rule of the product.

For governments

Built for the bar
a citizen helpline actually requires.

Voca is engineered, not retrofitted, for the realities of an Indian government deployment: data residency, audit, zero source-system modification, procurement-readiness. The architectural choices are deliberate.

01

On Indian foundation models

STT on AI4Bharat IndicConformer (IIT Madras). LLM interpretation on Sarvam-1. TTS on Indic-Parler-TTS. Self-hosted. No PII to hosted LLMs, ever. Bhashini wired in as a government-blessed fallback.

02

Audit-logged, end to end

Every state transition — Heard → Interpreted → Verified → Acted → Escalated — writes an immutable event with timestamps, model versions, payload hashes, audio chunk references. Officer-retrievable per call ID in ≤ 5 seconds.

03

No source-system modification

Voca is a parallel assistive layer. The 1092 telephony stack stays untouched. We tap call audio via SIP fork or LiveKit's media bridge and send interpretation events to the agent console. Existing systems are unmodified.

Procurement-readiness at a glance

  • On-prem deployable — Karnataka State Data Center compatible
  • Sovereign cloud option — runs inside Indian-only regions
  • Data residency — citizen audio never leaves India
  • WORM audit storage — immutable, 7-year retention configurable
  • Bhashini integration — government's blessed fallback path
  • GeM-ready architecture — designed for Government e-Marketplace
  • 22 official languages — language-agnostic orchestration
  • Graceful degradation — never silent failure; always hand to a human
  • SLO-bounded — P95 turn ≤ 900 ms, 99.95% voice path availability
  • Indian-vendor preference compliant — Indian foundation models throughout

Three moats. Compounding.

01

Dialect data flywheel

Every verification outcome and agent edit is a labeled data point. After 12 months in production, Voca holds the largest labeled corpus of dialect-tagged Indian citizen speech outside the AI4Bharat lab. Not replicable without years of operational data.

02

Government procurement lock-in

Once on GeM with two state deployments, every subsequent state defaults to the existing approved vendor. Classic switching-cost moat plus a regulatory moat. Indian government procurement velocity favors first movers heavily.

03

Verify-before-act product DNA

The product's design center — verify before act — is a philosophy, not a feature. Competitors copying the surface miss the underlying FSM and audit story. Hard to replicate with a different starting frame.
