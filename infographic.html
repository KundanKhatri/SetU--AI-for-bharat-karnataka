Voice-to-voice AI that ensures being understood comes before being answered.

Setu hears the citizen in her dialect, restates back what it understood, then acts. When confidence drops or distress spikes, it hands off to a human — with full context, sentiment timeline, and a suggested opening line.

Only the Verified state ever triggers an action. No interpretation, however confident, is acted upon without explicit citizen confirmation — except when distress + risk_signal both exceed threshold, in which case Setu rapid-acks and hands off to a human in parallel.

Three-layer dialect handling

  • Acoustic. AI4Bharat IndicConformer · 22 Indic langs · n-best hypotheses preserved · dialect-tagged training data.
  • Lexical. Sarvam-1 fine-tuned on a curated grievance corpus · Indic-native tokenizer (3–4× more efficient than GPT on Devanagari/Kannada).
  • Cultural. Honorific normalizer · curated idiom dictionary · district-entity gazetteer (pgvector). Deterministic — code, not stochastic.

Verification patterns by confidence

  • ≥ 0.85 · Single-shot paraphrase: "You're saying X. Right?"
  • 0.6 – 0.85 · Open-ended: "You're calling because Y. Could you tell me what I missed?"
  • < 0.6 · No paraphrase. One clarifying question. After 2 misses → human takeover.
