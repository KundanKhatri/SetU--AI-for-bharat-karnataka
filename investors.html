Voca is the application layer for India's voice future — vertical applications on Indian foundation models. The wedge is government helplines. The platform is every Indian helpline that needs to understand a citizen in their own language.

The market

small enough to be credible.

India runs roughly 4.5 billion helpline calls a year — government, BFSI, telco, healthcare. Every one of them is a multilingual problem. The numbers below are honest and bottom-up.

Defensibility
Three moats. Compounding.

A horizontal voice play wins or loses on defensibility. Voca's defensibility is not one big moat — it is three smaller moats that reinforce each other over time.

01

Dialect data flywheel

Every verification outcome and agent edit is a labeled data point. After 12 months in production, Voca holds the largest labeled corpus of dialect-tagged Indian citizen speech outside the AI4Bharat lab. Not replicable without years of operational data.

02

Government procurement lock-in

Once on GeM with two state deployments, every subsequent state defaults to the existing approved vendor. Classic switching-cost moat plus a regulatory moat. Indian government procurement velocity favors first movers heavily.

03

Verify-before-act product DNA

The product's design center — verify before act — is a philosophy, not a feature. Competitors copying the surface miss the underlying FSM and audit story. Hard to replicate with a different starting frame.

Why now
Three step-changes made this buildable
in the last 18 months.
2024–2025

Indic foundation models matured

AI4Bharat (IIT Madras) and Sarvam AI shipped open, dialect-aware models that didn't exist before. The acoustic gap on rural Kannada and Hindi closed from "frustrating" to "usable." Voca's STT and TTS were research-grade in 2024. They are deployable in 2026.

2024–2026

Real-time voice infra commoditized

LiveKit Agents, Pipecat, sub-100ms streaming TTS — research-grade in 2024, plumbing in 2026. A two-person team can plug them together in two weeks. Latency budgets that required custom engineering are now off-the-shelf.

2025–2026

Government readiness

Bhashini operational and open for integration. GeM ready to procure AI services that respect Indian sovereign-cloud requirements. Five years ago this would have died at the procurement gate. Now there is a path.

Roadmap
